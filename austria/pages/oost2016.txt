== 1. Liga (Second Level)  ‹§1liga›

<!-- $table3$ - Final Table -->

[*] FC Liefering (farm team RB Salzburg) are not eligible for promotion

=== Promotion Playoff  ‹§2prorel›

NB: both SV Austria Salzburg (who opened insolvency proceedings during the season) and
    SK Austria Klagenfurt (who were denied a second level license for next season in
    spite of two appeals) are forcibly relegated; as in addition SV Grödig (relegated
    from the first level) voluntarily return to the regional level, Floridsdorfer AC
    remain at the second level and three clubs are promoted from the regional leagues:
    SV Horn (Regionalliga Ost), FC Blau-Weiß Linz (Regionalliga Mitte) and WSG Wattens
    (Regionalliga West); the playoff for one promotion place between FC Blau-Weiß Linz
    and WSG Wattens will therefore not be played.
