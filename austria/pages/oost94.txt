Note: the Wiener Sport-Club and the SV Gerasdorf merged into a
Spielgemeinschaft at the end of the season, one team, as WSC/Gerasdorf,
competing in the 2. Bundesliga 1994/95 (which was WSC's place by
right, but this team plays at Gerasdorf home ground) and one in the

WSC's homeground in Dornbach).

Feeder Leagues for Regionalliga Ost:
-----------------------------------

<!-- $table10$ - Landesliga Burgenland -->

Promoted to Burgenländer Liga: Jennersdorf, Steinbrunn, and Horitschon.

<!-- $table11$ - Landesliga Niederösterreich -->

Promoted to Niederösterreicher Liga: Kottingbrunn and Retz (16 teams 94/95).

Wiener Liga


<!-- $table12$ -->

Promoted to Wiener Liga: Kaiserebersdorf, WAF Leopoldstadt, Fortuna 05.

Note: the Wiener Sport-Club and the SV Gerasdorf merged into a
Spielgemeinschaft at the end of the season, one team, as WSC/Gerasdorf,
competing in the 2. Bundesliga 1994/95 (which was WSC's place by
right, but this team plays at Gerasdorf home ground) and one in the
Ostliga as Gerasdorf/WSC (Gerasdorf's place by right, but playing at
WSC's homeground in Dornbach).
