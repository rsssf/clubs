== 2.Division


<!-- $table3$ -->

Promoted to the 2.Division:
Vorwärts Steyr (Mitte)
ASV Stockerau (Ost)
SPG Innsbruck (West)

After the season Schwarz-Weiß Bregenz and FC Dornbirn formed the
Spielgemeinschaft Bregenz/Dornbirn.
SPG Innsbruck was a Spielgemeinschaft formed by Austria Innsbruck,
SV Innsbruck and Innsbrucker Sportklub.
