== 2.Division


<!-- $table3$ -->

The SPG Innsbruck, formed by Austria Innsbruck, SV Innsbruck and
Innsbrucker Sportklub, was dissolved at the end of the season.

Promoted to the 2.Division:
SV Spittal/Drau (Mitte)
Vorwärts Steyr (Mitte)
Badener AC (Ost)
UFC Purbach (Ost)
Bregenz/Dornbirn (West)
SK Zell/See (West)
